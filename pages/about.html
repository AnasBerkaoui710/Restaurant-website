Our Story

A journey of passion, flavor, and culinary excellence

Crafting Memories Since 1998

Founded by the Al-Berkaoui family, Gusto Restaurant started as a small corner bistro with a simple vision: to serve authentic, soul-warming food made from the heart.

Over the past two decades, we have evolved into a destination for food lovers who appreciate the fine balance between traditional techniques and modern culinary innovation. Every dish we serve tells a story of heritage, sourced from the finest local ingredients.

Our mission is simple: to provide an exquisite culinary experience that nourishes both the body and the soul.

Fresh Ingredients

We work directly with local farmers to ensure only the freshest organic produce reaches your plate.

Made with Love

Our chefs pour their passion into every recipe, ensuring a consistent and delightful taste experience.

Quality Service

Hospitality is at our core. We treat every guest like family, providing attentive and professional service.

Meet Our Master Chef

Chef Marcus Sterling brings over 25 years of international culinary experience to our kitchen. Having trained in the Michelin-starred kitchens of Paris and Rome, Marcus specializes in fusion cuisine that respects classic roots.

"Cooking is an art form that should be accessible to everyone. At Gusto, we strive to create high-end flavors in an atmosphere that feels like home."
